Environment Poster

For my environmental poster i went through a few different ideas, originally i wanted to do an ocean based poster highlighting the issues presented when we dump our trash into the oceam, howevere after looking around on pixabay etc. i could not find an image i was happy basing my poster around, i attempted to make one based on an image of a volunteer helping to clean up the beaches but ultimatley i gave up and moved on.



My next idea was to base it upon deforestation in which i would find a visually moving image and use powerful wording along with the imagery to make the consumer stop and really think about the message the poster is converying, i found a good one of pixabay which was copyright free and safe to use, so i put in into photoshop and made my first iteration



After getting the base look of the poster and main wording i experimented with adding in some meaningfull facts into the poster without overshadowing anything, this meant finding the ccorrect font and font size so i layed out all the fonts i liked and compared how they looked on the page till i found one that fit




Eventually i found a font i liked and settled on that fit with the theme and tone of the poster and tied it all together to create the finished product below
